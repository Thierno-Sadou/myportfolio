Basketball Universe

  • A game coded in Python using the pygames module

  • The goal was to implement realistic trajectory calculation using real physic equations

  • It is a fun game with multiple levels of difficulty taking place in different places of the universe

Be Healthy

  • A project combining web programming and prevention

  • This site is the result of extensive research on the various physical and dietary habits necessary for good health

  • Has personalized forms and dynamic tables for the different calculations of BMI or daily intakes

Harmony is Digital

  • The goal of this project was to play nursery rhymes while drawing colorful patterns on the screen

  • The program reads the scores in text form, analyzes them and plays the right frequencies in rhythm

  • It could also accept as input new scores that it retained and could play again later

FisHelp

  • The goal of this project was to automatise the process of taking care of fish

  • The aquarium can measure temperature, pH and water quality and adapt them automatically

  • It can also feed the fish at a set frequency

  • The user is notified when it is time to change the water or when the fishes need to be fed

Travel Insight

  • This project, which is still under development, is designed to make travel organization easier

  • The site covers over 10,000 destinations around the world and offers a complete list of information to know such as optimal periods, entry policy or history.

  • It offers a complete and illustrated list of activities to do on site.

  • It also includes the possibility to book hotels depending on your budget and your programm
